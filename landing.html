Frequently Asked Questions

Is SimpleTodo really free?

Yes! The basic version of SimpleTodo is completely free to use with no time limits. We offer a Pro plan with additional features for power users.

How secure is my data?

We take security seriously. All data is encrypted and stored securely using Google's Firebase infrastructure.

Can I access SimpleTodo on my phone?

SimpleTodo works on any device with a web browser. Simply bookmark the page on your mobile device for easy access.

How do I get support?

Free users can access our help center. Pro users receive priority email support.
